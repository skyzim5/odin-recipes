Mushroom Gnocchi

Description

Mushroom and spinach gnocchi in a creamy cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 package gnocchi
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook gnocchi in the boiling water until they float to the top, 2 to 4 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Add sliced mushrooms and diced onions then cook until softened, about 3 minutes. Add garlic and continue to cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Reduce heat, and pour in cream.
  3. Place spinach in the bottom of a colander and drain the gnocchi over the spinach to wilt it.
  4. Stir cheese into the skillet and keep stirring until cheese is melted.
  5. Add gnocchi and wilted spinach to the skillet and stir until well combined. Serve and enjoy!
