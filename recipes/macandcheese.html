Description

Mac and Cheese with Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 1 package mac and cheese
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook macaroni in the boiling water for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile cut broccoli into desired size.
  3. Steam broccoli in a pan until softened as desired.
  4. When macaroni is cooked, drain water and return to pan adding butter and milk. Then slowly stir in cheese packet until evenly coated
  5. Once broccoli is cooked tranfer to macaroni pan and stir until lightly mixed.
