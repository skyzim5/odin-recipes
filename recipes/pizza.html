Mushroom, pepperoni, and olive pizza

Description

Mushroom, pepperoni, and olive pizza

Ingredients

  • premade pizza crust
  • 1 pack pizza pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 sliced olives
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Spread pizza sauce on pizza crust covering with a thin layer.
  3. Place pepperoni slices evenly all over.
  4. Place mushrooms slices evenly all over.
  5. Place olives evenly all over.
  6. Sprinkle cheese covering ingerdients until no big gaps are visible.
